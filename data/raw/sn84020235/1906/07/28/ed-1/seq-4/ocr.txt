games. The boys feel quite confident

players, whose term of enlNtment has

The L. N. D. S. club and a few of
their friends enjoyed a very pleasant
outing Thursday, the 11th, by spend
ing the day at the Old Folks’ home.
The ladies took dinner at the home,
which had been prepared by the pres
ident, Mrs. Casey, and her able corps
of workers. Words of welcome and
appreciation w’ere spoken by the pres
ident, Mrs. Casey, and Mrs. Joseph
Smith, Mrs. Ross and others. The
club returned to the city much
pleased with their outing, after hav
ing assisted the board by each one
buying their dinner.
