SONS ANO DAUGHTERS
OF REBECCA ENTERTAIN
The Sons and Daughters of Rebec
ca gave an entertainment last Mon
day night at Shelia’s hall. It was a
grand affair. Mrs. Mary Wilson, of
141 Morgan street, was conducting
the affair. The several societies
marched in, and Capt. C. H. Tandy
was called for and made a grand talk.
J. W. Wheeler was introduced and
presented the several prizes to the
different societies that were out, after
which the guests enjoyed themselves
to a late hour, while our reporter no
ticed many things. Little Misses Hel
en Hargot and Edith Stevens were
kept busy.
