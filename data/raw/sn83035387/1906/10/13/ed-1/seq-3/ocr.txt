the government, now has under bond
all of the seven men recently indicted
on charges of ]k'onage in connection

corn is one of the best remedies for
nausea caused by seasickness or car

before many hours had passed, at
tacked by the usual symptoms. She
decided to try the new remedy, what
ever it might be, that she had been
assured she would find in a good sizec
box that had been given her at her

traveler tried the popcorn she found
in the box, and to her gratification the
nausea gradually disappeared. She
ate freely of the corn every day when
ever there was the slightest indication
of nausea, and thanks to this simple
precaution thn journey Wad accom
plished with lui unwonted degree of
comfort. —Harper’s Bazar.
