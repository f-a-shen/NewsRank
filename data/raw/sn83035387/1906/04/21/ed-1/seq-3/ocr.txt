Mr. Donald Gibbs, a son of Judge M.
W. Gibbs, and a brother of Mr. Hor
ace Gibbs, of Lorain, well known in
this city and Oberlin where he lived
for many years before going into the
northwest some years ago, died at
Tacoma, Washington, April after
many years Illness. For six months
prior to his death ,Mr. Gibbs apparent
ly improved, the result being that his
death was unexpected. “Don,” as he
was familiarly called by hosts of local
friends, was a fine mechanic, a big
hearted manly man, intelligent and
lovable. He was our friend. Inter
ment in Tacoma. His relatives have
our sincerest sympathy.
