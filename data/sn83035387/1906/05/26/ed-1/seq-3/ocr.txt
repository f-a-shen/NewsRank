Harry C. Smith wgs the event of the
। evening (Tuesday.) The gentleman
I spoke upon the subject ’Race Leaders,
I Who are They? Who Should They
Be.’ He very ably and most eloquent
ly pointed out the one weak point of
the race—lack of crystalized leader
ship. He told his audience of the
great men of the past—who had all
■ the qualities of leadership—but who
I could not lead because our people
