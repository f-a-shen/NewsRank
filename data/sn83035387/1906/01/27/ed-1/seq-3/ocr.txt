; gence from that point since the flood
.of Tuesday. The downpour at Marion

Bishop Turner's Wife Dead
Philadelphia. Pa.—Mrs. Harriet A.
Turner, wife of Bishop Henry M.
Turner, and widow of Bishop A. W.
Wayman, died at the home of Mr. and
Mrs. Walter P. Hall on Jan. 10. Mrs.
Turner had been an invalid for some
time, and had lived in Philadelphia
because the climate of Atlanta did not
agree with her. She was married to
Bishop Turner several years ago in
Baltimore. The bishop was in Sa
vannah. Ga., at the time of his wife's
death attending the general confer
ence commission.
