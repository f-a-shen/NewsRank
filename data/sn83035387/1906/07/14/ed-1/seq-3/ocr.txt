so far been found. The wages are
$1.60 to $2 a day.

Laura Clay, 24, Afro-American, and
Joe Leberak, 64, French Canadian,
came to Cleveland Thursday from
Bloomfield, to get married, but failed
because Deputy Zizelman couldn’t
identify the groom-elect. While the
groom tried to get the license, the
bride waited at the office of Justice
Wm. Brown. “I won’t go back to that
farm again until I am his wife,” she
said. "I have worked hard enough
for him. See those corns on my
hands,” and she held them up for in
spection. “I got those working on one
of Joe’s farms. He’s got money and
owns two farms. I’ll have a good
home and an easy time. Joe look#
old, but he is just as young as he
ever was.” The number of white men
who marry Afro-American women
steadily increase throughout the
country. The same is true of white
women and male Afro-Americans.
Thus doth Charles W. Chesnutt’s
amalgamation prediction evolve.
