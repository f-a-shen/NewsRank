Conference a Grand Success.
Piqua, O. —The conference of the
Springfield district of the northern
Ohio conference of the great A. M. E.
church is and has been in session here
nearly all week. It will close to
morrow. That strong race leader, Rt.
Rev. W. B. Derrick, is the presiding
bishop, and Rev. Dr. J. M. Gilmere,
the aggressive and tireless worker for
the cause of Christ and the church, is
the presiding elder. He has in circu
lation beautiful programs (printed by
The Gazettei and all is moving on
splendidly, just as anticipated. The
veteran pastor. Rev. Dr. Coleman, and
his congregation are taking care of
the conference nicely in every way.
