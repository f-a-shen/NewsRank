Last week we said that we will soon
run out some of the worse Negroes in
St. Louis, of whom some were preach
ers, doctors, etc. One old bandy
shank Negro woman said to a gentle
man: “Dat man better not say any
thing about my preacher or I’m gwine
rite down dere and whip him, for my
preacher is all right.” This is the
trouble now. A lot of old handker
chief-headed, bandy shank Negroes
that haven’t sense enough to carry
corn to a dead bear without getting
scratched. They had better serve
God than continue serving man.
SPIRITUAL CHRISTIAN UNION.
The Spiritual Christian Union is
to draw together all persons in bands
of love and sympathy, uplifting all
out of darkness into light, showing all
the true light, the giver of all life.
Those desirous of living on a higher
plane are asked to join us. Come
with cheerful hearts overflowing with
love for ethers, thereby receiving a
blessing in return. Lectures and
tests by J. S. Weatherford and Mrs.
E. Brooks at the Masonic building
2720 Morgan street every Friday even
ing 8 p. m. All are welcome.
